**7.Digital Library Search and Recommendation Engine**									

**Bloom Filter:** Implement to quickly verify if a book has been accessed recently without storing access logs.			

**Behavior-Based Sampling:** Sample user activities based on their reading patterns to provide personalized recommendations.


**HyperLogLog:** Estimate the number of unique visitors to the library.									

**Frequency Moments (Moment 1):** Analyze the frequency of genre preferences among users.				

**DGIM Algorithm:** Track the number of times a particular book is accessed within a specific timeframe.
